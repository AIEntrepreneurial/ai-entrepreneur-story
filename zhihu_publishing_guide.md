# Guide: How to Publish the AI Article on Zhihu

## Steps to Publish:

1. Log in to your Zhihu account at zhuanlan.zhihu.com
2. Click on "开始写文章" (Start Writing) button
3. Choose a compelling title for the article
4. Format the content appropriately for Zhihu readers
5. Add relevant tags such as: 人工智能, 科技, 未来, 创新
6. Review and publish

## Content Formatting Tips for Zhihu:

- Use clear headings and subheadings
- Break up long paragraphs into shorter, digestible sections
- Include relevant images or diagrams if possible
- End with questions to encourage engagement
- Use a conversational tone that resonates with Zhihu's educated audience
- Ensure the content adds value and encourages discussion
